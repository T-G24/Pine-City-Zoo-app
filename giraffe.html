GIRAFFE

Giraffes are the tallest land animals, known for their long necks and unique spotted patterns. They are native to Africa and primarily feed on leaves from tall trees.

At Pine City Zoo, you can find the Giraffe exhibit in the African Savanna section. This area is designed to replicate their natural habitat, providing ample space for these gentle giants to roam and graze.
